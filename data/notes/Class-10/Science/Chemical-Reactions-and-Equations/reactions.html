What is a Chemical Reaction?

In our daily life, we observe many changes happening around us. Some changes are temporary, while others lead to the formation of new substances. When a change results in the formation of one or more new substances, it is called a chemical reaction.

Daily Life Examples

  • Milk turning sour when kept at room temperature
  • Rusting of an iron nail in moist air
  • Cooking of food
  • Digestion of food in our body
  • Respiration

In all these cases, the original substance changes into a new substance with different properties. This indicates that a chemical reaction has taken place.

Definition

A chemical reaction is a process in which one or more substances (reactants) are transformed into new substances (products).

Simple Example

When magnesium ribbon is burned in air, it reacts with oxygen to form magnesium oxide.

  • Chemical reactions are part of everyday life
